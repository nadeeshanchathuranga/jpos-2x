Expenses

Track business expenses related to inventory and operations.

Creating an Expense

  1. Navigate to Purchasing & Stock ‚Üí Expenses
  2. Click "Create New Expense" or "Add Expense"
  3. Fill in the expense details:
    • Supplier: Select the supplier related to this expense (if applicable)
    • Date: Date when the expense occurred (required)
    • Total Amount: Total expense amount in LKR (required)
    • Paid: Amount already paid in LKR
    • Balance(LKR): Remaining balance to be paid
    • Amount(LKR): Payment amount being made (required)
    • Payment Type: Payment method - Cash, Check, Bank Transfer, etc. (required)
  4. Review all information for accuracy
  5. Click "Create Expense" to record the expense
