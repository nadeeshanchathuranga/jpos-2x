Suppliers

Manage your supplier database for streamlined purchasing.

Adding a Supplier

  1. Go to Purchasing & Stock ‚Üí Suppliers
  2. Click "Add Supplier"
  3. Enter supplier details:
    • Supplier Name: Company name (required)
    • Contact Person: Primary contact
    • Email: Email address
    • Phone: Contact number
    • Address: Physical address
    • City/Country: Location
    • Payment Terms: Net 30, Net 60, COD, etc.
    • Bank Details: For payments
  4. Save the supplier
