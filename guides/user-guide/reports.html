Reports & Analytics

Generate comprehensive reports to analyze your business performance.

Sales Reports

Sales summary by type with income details. View comprehensive sales analytics with key metrics, payment breakdowns, and detailed product sales information.

Generating a Sales Report

  1. Navigate to Reports ‚Üí Sales Report
  2. Set the date range:
    • From Date: Select start date (e.g., 12/01/2025)
    • To Date: Select end date (e.g., 12/17/2025)
  3. Click "Apply" to generate the report
  4. Click "Reset" to clear filters and start over

Key Metrics Displayed

The report displays three main summary cards:

  • Total Income: Total revenue from all sales in the period
  • Total Sales: Number of sales transactions
  • Net After Returns: Total income minus returns and adjustments

Income by Payment Type

Shows a breakdown of income received through different payment methods:

  • Cash: Cash payments received with transaction count
  • Card: Card payments (if applicable)
  • Check: Check payments (if applicable)
  • Bank Transfer: Electronic transfers (if applicable)

Each payment type card shows the total amount and number of transactions.

Sales by Type Table

Detailed breakdown of sales by type (Retail, Wholesale, etc.)

Columns:

  • Type: Sale type (Retail, Wholesale, etc.)
  • Sales Count: Number of transactions of this type
  • Gross Total: Total before discounts
  • Discount: Total discounts applied
  • Net Total: Amount after discounts
  • Returns: Amount from returned items
  • Net After Returns: Final amount after all adjustments
  • Balance: Any remaining balance

Export Options: Click "Export PDF" or "Export Excel" to download the table

Product Sales & Returns Report

Detailed product-level sales information including returns

Columns:

  • Product Name: Name of the product sold
  • Barcode: Product barcode/SKU
  • Sales Date: Date of the sale
  • Sales Qty: Quantity sold
  • Sales Amount: Total amount for that product
  • Sales Return Date: Date of return (if applicable)
  • Returns Qty: Quantity returned
  • Returns Amount: Amount of returned items
  • Net Sales Qty: Final quantity after returns
  • Net Sales Amount: Final amount after returns

Export Options: Click "Export PDF" or "Export Excel" to download the detailed table

Using Sales Reports

  • Generate daily reports to monitor sales performance
  • Compare periods to identify trends and growth
  • Track payment method preferences
  • Analyze return rates and patterns
  • Export for tax and accounting purposes
  • Share with management for performance review

Product Stock Reports

Monitor current stock levels, stock values, and inventory health.

Generating a Stock Report

  1. Go to Reports ‚Üí Stock Report
  2. Select filters:
    • Category
    • Brand
    • Stock status (Low, Out of Stock, etc.)
  3. Click "Generate Report"
  4. Review stock information
  5. Export if needed

Stock Report Columns

  • Product: Product name
  • SKU: Product code
  • Category: Product category
  • Current Stock: Quantity on hand
  • Minimum Level: Reorder point
  • Unit Price: Cost price
  • Total Value: Stock value
  • Status: In stock, Low, Out of stock
